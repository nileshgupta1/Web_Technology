Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Bacon
  • Butter

Instructions

1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
2. Assemble sandwich with bread, cheese, tomato, and bacon.
3. Spread butter on the outside of the bread.
4. Cook sandwich in the skillet until bread is golden brown and cheese is melted.
5. Serve and enjoy!
